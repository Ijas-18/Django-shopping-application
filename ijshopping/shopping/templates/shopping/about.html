About

This is a website where you can buy books and make it sent to your E-mail.

  • In Home you can see the list of books available.
  • In Profile you can see and update your details.
  • In Order you can see all of your orders made in this site.
  • If you are a new user click Register to create a new account or click LogIn to login to your account.


    Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ)

    • Go to Home to see the list of books available
    • Select the book you need and you will see the details of the book
    • Click Buy Now button below description to make an order
    • On the next page click Yes and wait for sometime
    • Finally, a success message will be displayed saying your order has been completed.

    • Go to LogIn and click forgot password or click Here
    • Enter your email you used to login and click on 'Request Password Reset'
    • A E-mail with a link to reset password will then be sent to your registerd E-mail Address
    • Click the link and Enter your new password
    • You can now be able to login with your new password
